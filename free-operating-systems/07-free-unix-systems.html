¶Free *nix Systems

Presently the most popular free *nix operating systems can be split into the following two categories:

  • *BSDs such as FreeBSD, NetBSD and OpenBSD and derivatives thereof.
  • Linux distributions

the remaining free *nix systems are less popular and include Redox. This post will pay vastly more attention to the Linux distributions and *BSDs, than these minority free *nix systems.
